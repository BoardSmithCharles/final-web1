My Favorite Skater Andy Anderson

Information On Andy

Anderson turned pro for Powell Peralta in 2019, and is currently sponsored by Powell Peralta, Etnies, Skull Skates, Mini Logo, and Bones, along with clothing brands such as, Swatch and Skullcandy.

In the final 2020 Olympic qualifying event (the Dew Tour 2021) Anderson tore his meniscus during a practice run.[13] Despite this, Anderson competed in the event finishing 11th in the semifinals upon achieving a score of 75.80 after two successful runs, qualifying for the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. After performing qualifying runs, his third and fourth runs of the event were lighthearted, including a 33-second long manual atop the park exterior
